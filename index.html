H2O - Hybridized Help Online

H2O is a tool designed to help teens with struggles in a simple and easy way. H2O uses a hybrid system of Algorithms, Neural Networks, and Human data to provide accurate and helpful insight into teen struggles.

Try our Prototype!

Our mission
Throughout the school years of one’s life, it’s common to encounter different problems that may seem daunting to solve, especially if they relate to your mental health. This problem is prevalent in teenagers, as many find it difficult to reach out and speak to others when it comes to emotional problems. Because of this, Hybridized Help Online aims to connect those in need to official resources as well as be an empathetic chatbot catered to specific personality types’ conversational styles.
